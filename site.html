About

This website is dedicated to the love-hate relationship shared throughout all Knicks fans. This website may offend some diehard Knicks fans, but they're gonna have to deal with it because they won't admit that I'm right

The New York Knicks (sometimes referred to as the Knickerbockers), are a basketball team based in New York City. Founded in 1946 by Ned Irish, the Knicks officially entered the league in 1949. Unlike the modern-day Knicks, the 1970 Knicks were a solid team. In the decade, they made the NBA Finals 3 times and won twice.
